Every item undergoes a rigorous authentication process including visual inspection by our expert team, material analysis, and comparison with verified reference pieces. We also verify the provenance and temple documentation. Each authentic item comes with a certificate of authenticity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PromptPay QR, bank transfer, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and cash on delivery for orders within Thailand. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption to protect your information.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping within Thailand takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. All items are carefully packaged with protective materials and insured during transit. Free shipping is available for orders over ฿5,000.

Can I return an item if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 7-day return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item in its original condition for a full refund. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
