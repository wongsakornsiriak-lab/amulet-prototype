Luang Phor Ngern of Wat Bang Khlan is one of the most revered monks in Thai Buddhist history. His sacred amulets are highly sought after by collectors and devotees worldwide for their powerful protective properties and historical significance.

This 1st edition coin represents one of the earliest consecrations from the temple, making it exceptionally rare and valuable. The coin displays the classic features associated with authentic Luang Phor Ngern pieces: clear facial details, proper weight, and characteristic patina developed over more than a century.

Each coin has been examined using traditional authentication methods as well as modern analysis techniques. Our certification process includes visual inspection, material analysis, and comparison with known authentic examples in our reference collection.
